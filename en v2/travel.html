eHaltAir

French company founded in 1996, eHaltAir is a flag carrier who gather passion for History and travel. The current CEO is Emmanuel HALTER.

Specialized in short-haul flights at the beginning, She places the world discovery as one of its main goal. Since 2011, the company has developed its international market including New York by example.

Reasons being of this society are simple : discover new cultures, have a differentworldview and exchange with people to be open-minded.

To answer to customer's needs, eHaltAir offers a new destination every three months. Its most famous travel formula is between 3 and 4 days.

Its motto sums up mindset of the company : "Whether near or far, the adventure begins at the foot of your bed"
